About Phishing Attacks

Phishing is a dangerous form of cyber attack where attackers create fake websites that look identical to trusted platforms such as banks, social media sites, or e-commerce platforms.

The primary goal of phishing is to steal sensitive information like usernames, passwords, OTPs, credit card details, banking information, and personal data.

These malicious websites often use techniques such as:

  • Fake domain names that resemble real websites
  • Suspicious URL patterns and hidden redirects
  • HTTPS misuse to appear secure
  • Social engineering to trick users into clicking links

Our Phishing Website Detection System uses Machine Learning algorithms to analyze URL patterns and predict whether a website is Legitimate or Phishing.
